Boba

Boba is a tea-based drink originating from Taiwan that features chewy tapioca balls or other toppings.
Also known as bubble tea or pearl milk tea, this beloved beverage has become a global phenomenon since its creation in the 1980s.
The drink typically consists of a tea base (black, green, or oolong), milk or fruit flavoring, sweetener, and the signature chewy tapioca pearls.
Boba comes in countless variations with different flavors, toppings like jelly or popping boba, and can be served hot or cold.
The unique texture and customizable nature of boba tea has made it a cultural icon and a favorite drink for millions worldwide.
